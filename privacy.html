1Policy Overview

Globiz is an Electron-based desktop application that helps B2B teams discover target customers, plan and execute outreach campaigns, capture engagement signals, and progress deals through AI-assisted workflows. In delivering these capabilities, we necessarily handle personal data — both yours as a user and data about the business contacts you manage within the platform.

This Privacy Policy applies to all data processing activities carried out by AlphaTok Singapore Pte. Ltd. in connection with the Globiz desktop application, our website, and any APIs or integrations we offer. It is written to comply with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA) and, where applicable, to align with the principles of the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the United States CAN-SPAM Act.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data to data brokers, advertisers, or other third parties for their own marketing use.

2Scope & Application

This Policy applies to:

  • Registered users of the Globiz desktop application, whether on a free trial, paid subscription, or enterprise plan;
  • Business contacts whose personal data is imported or generated within the platform by Globiz users;
  • Visitors to our website and marketing pages; and
  • Prospective customers who contact us about our products.

This Policy does not apply to the privacy practices of third-party platforms integrated with Globiz (e.g., LinkedIn, Twitter/X, WhatsApp). Those platforms have their own privacy policies, which you should review independently.

Note for EU/UK users: Where you access Globiz from within the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, we apply GDPR-equivalent protections in addition to our PDPA obligations. The relevant lawful bases and your enhanced rights are detailed in Section 8 and Section 13.

3Our Role as Data Controller and Data Processor

When we are the Data Controller

We act as the data controller for personal data we collect directly from you — such as your account registration details, billing information, usage analytics, and customer support communications. We determine the purposes and means of processing this data.

When we are the Data Processor

We act as a data processor for the personal data of third-party contacts (leads, customers, prospects) that you import or create within Globiz's CRM. In this capacity, we process that data solely on your instructions and in accordance with our Data Processing Agreement. You, the Globiz user, are the data controller for those contacts and are responsible for ensuring you have a lawful basis to process and store their personal data.

Your responsibility: Before importing third-party contact data into Globiz (e.g., from a spreadsheet, LinkedIn export, or third-party tool), you must ensure you have a valid lawful basis to hold and process that data under applicable law, including the PDPA and any laws in your contacts' jurisdictions.

7Data We Collect — Device & Local Storage

Globiz is a desktop application that stores data locally on your device. The following data resides in ~/.globizwork/:

  • Main database (globizwork.sqlite) — CRM records, campaigns, pipeline data;
  • Thread checkpoints (threads/) — AI agent state and conversation history for resumability;
  • API credentials (.env) — your third-party API keys (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, etc.); and
  • OAuth state (linkedin-state.json, similar files) — authentication tokens for connected social platforms.

You are responsible for the physical and logical security of your device and local storage. We recommend encrypting your device's hard drive and using strong system-level authentication.

8Data We Collect — Communications Data

When you communicate with us directly (e.g., by emailing support or submitting a bug report), we collect:

  • Your name and email address;
  • The content of your message; and
  • Any attachments or screenshots you voluntarily share.

We use this data solely to respond to your enquiry and improve the Service. Support communications are retained for up to 2 years.

9Data We Collect — Payment Data

Subscription payments are processed by a third-party payment processor (e.g., Stripe). We do not store your full card number, CVV, or bank account details. We retain:

  • Billing name and address;
  • Last four digits of card (for display only);
  • Transaction IDs and amounts; and
  • Subscription status and history.

Payment data is retained for at least 7 years to meet Singapore accounting and tax record obligations.

10Data We Collect — AI Interaction Data

Globiz routes certain inputs to large language model (LLM) providers (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI GPT, Google Gemini) to power AI features. The following data may be transmitted to and processed by those providers:

  • Prompts and instructions you enter into the AI agent chat;
  • CRM context snippets used to ground AI responses (e.g., contact name, company, deal stage); and
  • Drafted outreach content submitted for AI editing or generation.
Important: Do not submit sensitive personal data (e.g., health information, government ID numbers, financial account numbers) to the AI agent interface. LLM providers process inputs on their own infrastructure under their own privacy policies. See Section 12 for details.

AI interaction logs (prompts and responses) are stored locally as part of the thread checkpoint system at ~/.globizwork/threads/. We retain server-side AI usage metadata (token counts, model used, latency) for billing and performance monitoring.

12Legal Bases for Processing (PDPA)

Under the Singapore PDPA, we rely on the following bases to collect, use, and disclose personal data:

Consent

Where you have given us explicit, voluntary consent — for example, when opting in to marketing communications or enabling optional telemetry features. You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting processing carried out before withdrawal.

Contractual Necessity

Processing necessary to perform the contract we have with you — delivering the Globiz Service, processing your subscription, and providing customer support.

Legitimate Interests

We process certain data for our legitimate business interests where those interests are not overridden by your rights and freedoms. This includes fraud prevention, security monitoring, product analytics (using anonymised data), and communicating service improvements. We conduct balancing tests to ensure this basis is appropriate.

Legal Obligation

Processing required to comply with applicable laws — including Singapore tax and accounting law, anti-money laundering regulations, and lawful requests from regulators or courts.

17Security Measures

We implement a multi-layered approach to data security:

Technical Controls

  • Encryption in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher for all data exchanged with our servers;
  • Encryption at rest for databases held on our cloud infrastructure;
  • API keys and OAuth tokens stored locally are handled by Electron secure storage mechanisms;
  • Regular security patching and dependency vulnerability scanning; and
  • Access controls and authentication enforced across our infrastructure.

Organisational Controls

  • Access to production systems is limited to authorised personnel on a need-to-know basis;
  • Staff are trained on data protection and security practices; and
  • We conduct periodic security assessments of our systems and sub-processors.

Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify the Singapore Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC) within 3 calendar days if the breach is notifiable under the PDPA. We will notify affected individuals without undue delay where the breach is likely to result in significant harm to them.

Your device security matters too. Because Globiz stores data locally at ~/.globizwork/, your device's physical and logical security is critical. We strongly recommend enabling full-disk encryption and using a strong system password. We cannot be responsible for breaches resulting from unauthorized access to your local device.
